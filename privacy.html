GDPR Compliance & Data Protection

Last updated: 20th September 2025

ClearFuneralCosts is committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK data protection laws. This section outlines your rights and our obligations under these regulations.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data:

  1. Right of Access: Request copies of your personal data
  2. Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  3. Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  4. Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  5. Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  6. Right to Object: Object to processing for specific purposes
  7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
  8. Rights regarding Automated Decision Making: Human intervention in automated processes

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@clearfuneralcosts.co.uk. We will respond within one month of your request, free of charge.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional services
  • Contract: To provide our funeral cost information services
  • Legitimate Interests: For service improvement and fraud prevention
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with CMA reporting requirements

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. When this occurs, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for data transfers to approved countries
  • UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA)
  • Binding Corporate Rules for transfers within corporate groups

Regulatory Compliance Framework

Cookie Policy

Last updated: 20th September 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how ClearFuneralCosts uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website clearfuneralcosts.co.uk.

Cookie Commitment

We use cookies responsibly to improve your experience while respecting your privacy. You have full control over cookie preferences and can modify them at any time.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience more efficient and personalised.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Session cookies: Maintain your session while browsing
  • Security cookies: Help identify and prevent security risks
  • Load balancing cookies: Ensure optimal website performance
  • Form cookies: Remember form progress to prevent data loss

Analytics Cookies (With Your Consent)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and performance (anonymised)
  • Internal analytics: Our own tracking for service optimisation
  • Heatmap tools: Understand user interaction patterns

Functional Cookies (With Your Consent)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation:

  • Preference cookies: Remember your choices and settings
  • Language cookies: Remember your language preference
  • Accessibility cookies: Remember accessibility preferences

Marketing Cookies (With Your Consent)

These cookies help us show relevant information about our services:

  • Conversion tracking: Measure effectiveness of information campaigns
  • Remarketing pixels: Show relevant information about our services on other websites
  • Social media pixels: Measure engagement from social platforms

Cookie Duration and Storage

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for up to 2 years
  • Analytics cookies: Typically stored for 26 months
  • Marketing cookies: Usually expire after 90 days

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Easy Cookie Management

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner allowing you to choose which types of cookies to accept. You can modify these preferences at any time using our cookie management tool.

Browser Cookie Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data

Third-Party Cookie Opt-Outs

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads: Google Ad Settings
  • Industry Opt-out: Your Online Choices

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Understanding how disabling different cookie types affects your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Website may not function properly (forms, security)
  • Analytics cookies: We can't improve our services based on usage data
  • Functional cookies: You may need to re-enter preferences each visit
  • Marketing cookies: You may see less relevant information about our services

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

  • Web beacons: Small transparent images that track email opens
  • Local storage: Stores data locally on your device for functionality
  • Server logs: Automatically collect basic usage information

Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • Updated "Last modified" date at the top of this policy
