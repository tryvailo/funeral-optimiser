Frequently Asked Questions

Was I charged for the failed payment?
No, if the payment failed, your card was not charged. You may see a temporary authorization that will be released by your bank within 3-5 business days.
Will I lose my questionnaire responses?
No, your responses are saved. When you retry payment, you'll proceed directly to checkout without needing to complete the questionnaire again.
How many times can I retry payment?
You can retry as many times as needed. However, multiple failed attempts may trigger additional security checks from your bank. If you experience repeated failures, contact your bank first.
Is it safe to retry my payment?
Yes, absolutely. All payments are processed securely through Stripe with SSL encryption and PCI compliance. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
Can I complete the purchase later?
Yes, your questionnaire data is saved for 7 days. You can return at any time to complete your purchase. We'll send you a reminder email with a direct payment link.
